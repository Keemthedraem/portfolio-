About me

Hello! My name is Mateen Hakeem, and I’m a passionate front-end web developer with a degree in Computer Science from the University of Houston. I specialize in creating responsive, user-friendly websites that bring ideas to life on the web. With a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern front-end frameworks with a little bit of Python I enjoy crafting sleek and efficient digital experiences.

I’m always striving to improve my skills and stay at the forefront of front-end development. My goal is to become a full-stack developer, bridging the gap between front-end and back-end technologies to create more comprehensive web solutions. I’m also interested in UI/UX design, and I hope to blend my technical skills with design principles to craft even more engaging and intuitive user experiences.

Pokedex is a fun little project I created using Python that converts JPG images to PNG format. It utilizes Python’s powerful image-processing libraries to ensure high-quality conversions while maintaining the original image properties. This project was a great way for me to practice working with file formats, automation, and basic image manipulation in Python.
